Cannabis Seized in Police Raid at Residential Flat in Pune

Published on April 10, 2025

Pune: In a late evening operation, Pune Police conducted a raid at a residential flat Siddhivinayak Ginger in the Pimple Saudagar area following a tip-off regarding alleged possession of illegal substances.

During the search, authorities reportedly recovered a small quantity of suspected cannabis from the premises. The raid was carried out after officers received specific intelligence inputs.

Police officials confirmed that the seized material has been sent for forensic examination. The individual found at the premises has been detained for questioning under relevant provisions of the Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances (NDPS) Act.

Authorities have stated that investigations are ongoing and further details will be released following confirmation from forensic reports.

Law enforcement agencies have urged citizens to report suspicious activities to maintain safety and law and order in residential communities.
